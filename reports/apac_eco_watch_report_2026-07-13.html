Executive summary
The APAC region (ex-Mainland China) presents a broadly stable macroeconomic environment for TLD Group, with no CRITICAL threshold breaches detected. Manufacturing PMIs are mixed but near the 50 threshold, with India and Vietnam showing expansion while Japan and South Korea remain subdued. The China+1 supply chain diversification trend is accelerating, with rising FDI in Vietnam, India, Thailand, and Indonesia, creating new opportunities for TLD's commercial footprint. The net impact on TLD's APAC operations is moderately positive, with the main priority being to capitalize on the China+1 trend and lower financing costs while preparing contingency plans for the identified risks.
